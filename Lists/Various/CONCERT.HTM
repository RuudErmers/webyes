A significant amount of the information used to compile this document has come from fellow Renaissance and Annie Haslam enthusiasts. Today this document contains a listing of known concerts and corresponding track listings. We want to make this document a complete historical listing of the artists' concerts. Our goal is to complete the date, venue, location, lineup and track list information. Due to the extensive amount of detective work required to achieve this goal, the document can only grow with further information and input from fans like you.

Please send your comments, corrections and additions to nanook@usit.net.
